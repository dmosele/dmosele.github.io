Section 7.5 Sinusoidal Functions

Worksheet 7.5.1 Worksheet

1.
For the following graphs of periodic functions, estimate the period, midline, and amplitude of the function. Then find the formula for the function. (Hint, the period is always either a multiple of \(\pi\) or a whole number).

  4. (challenge)

Solution
  1. The period is \(\pi\text{,}\) the midline is \(y=2\text{,}\) the amplitude is \(4\text{,}\) and a possible formula is \(y=4\sin(2x)+2\)
  2. The period is \(2\text{,}\) the midline is \(y=-1\text{,}\) the amplitude is \(1\text{,}\) and a possible formula is \(y=\cos(\pi x)-1\)
  3. The period is \(\frac{2\pi}{3}\text{,}\) the midline is \(y=10\text{,}\) the amplitude is \(20\text{,}\) and a possible formula is \(y=-20\cos(3 x)+10\)
  4. The period is \(2\pi\text{,}\) the midline is \(y=2\text{,}\) the amplitude is \(3\text{,}\) and a possible formula is \(y=3\cos( x+0.5)+2\text{.}\) This one has a shift left by 0.5.
