Worksheet Exercise 1.
Surprise! You receive a $5000 bonus from your new job! You are deciding how you should invest this. (You really want to buy a new motorcycle.)
  • Option 1: A savings account that earns $12.1%$ compounded annually.
  • Option 2: A savings account that earns $12%$ compounded monthly.
  1. Suppose that we invest in "Option 1". Write a formula for the balance of the account \(A\) after \(t\) years.
  2. Suppose that we invest in "Option 2". Write a formula for the balance of the account \(B\) after \(t\) years.
  3. What is the effective interest rate for "Option 2"? Which account would you invest in?

  4. The motorcycle you want is $10000. How long will it take to save up that much using "Option 2"?
