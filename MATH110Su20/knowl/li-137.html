Item 5.1.1.1.e.

\(\log(\sqrt{1000})=\frac 32\) There are several ways to approach this, but using the fact that square root is the same as the \(\frac 12 \) power,

\begin{equation*} \log(\sqrt{1000}) = \log(1000^{\frac 12}) = \frac 12 \log(1000) = \frac 12 \cdot 3 = \frac 32 \end{equation*}
