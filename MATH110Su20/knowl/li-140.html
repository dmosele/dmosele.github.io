Item 5.2.1.2.a.

We start with what we know, namely that \(P=11000b^t\) as the initial quantity is 11000. We then use the fact that \(P\) is 13000 when \(t=3\) to see

\begin{equation*} 13000=11000b^3 \quad \Rightarrow \quad \frac{13000}{11000} = b^3 \quad \Rightarrow \quad \sqrt[3]{\frac{13000}{11000}} = b \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} P = 11000\left( \sqrt[3]{\frac{13000}{11000}} \right)^t \end{equation*}
