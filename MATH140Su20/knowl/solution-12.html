Solution 2.2.1.2.1.
Using the limit definition we get
\begin{align*} f'(x) \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{f(x+h)-f(x)}{h} \\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{(x+h)^2-4(x+h)+5-(x^2-4x+5)}{h}\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{x^2+2hx+h^2-4x-4h+5-x^2+4x-5}{h}\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{2hx+h^2-4h}{h} \\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} \frac{h(2x+h-4)}{h}\\ \amp = \lim_{h\to 0} 2x+h-4 = 2x-4 \end{align*}
