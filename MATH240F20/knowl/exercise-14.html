Worksheet Exercise 1.

Let \(\vec u = 3\vec i + \vec j - \vec k\) and \(\vec v = \vec i - 4\vec j + 2 \vec k\text{.}\)

  1. Compute \(\vec u \times \vec v\text{.}\)
  2. Check that it is indeed perpendicular to \(\vec u\) and \(\vec v\text{.}\)
  3. What can you say about \(\vec v \times \vec u\text{?}\)
