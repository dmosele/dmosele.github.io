Worksheet Exercise 1.

Let \(f(x,y) = e^{x+2y} + y\sin(x)\text{.}\)

  1. Compute all 4 second order partials of \(f\) and verify that \(f_{xy}=f_{yx}\text{.}\)
  2. Compute the equation of the tangent plane to \(f\) at \((0,0)\text{.}\)
  3. Compute the quadratic Taylor polynomial of \(f\) about \((0,0)\text{.}\)
  4. Estimate \(f(0.1,-0.1)\) using (i) linear approximation (tangent plane approximation) and (ii) the quadratic Taylor polynomial and compare it to the real value of \(f(0.1,-0.1)\text{.}\) Before you compute it, which estimate do you expect to be more accurate?
