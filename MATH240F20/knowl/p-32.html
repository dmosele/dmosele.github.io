Let \(f(x,y) = x^2+2y^2\text{.}\)

  1. Compute the critical points of \(f(x,y)\text{.}\)
  2. Compute the ``special points" as identified by the method of Lagrange multipliers subject to the constraint that \(g(x,y)=x^2+y^2=4\text{.}\)
  3. Find the maximum and minimum values of \(f(x,y)\) on the disk \(x^2+y^2 \leq 4\text{.}\)
