Jane wanders slowly down a circular staircase. With \(x\text{,}\) \(y\text{,}\) and \(z\) in feet and the ground floor on the plane \(z=0\text{,}\) her position \(t\) minutes from the start is given by

\begin{equation*} x = 12 \cos t, \hspace{.3in} y = 12 \sin t, \hspace{.3in} z = 100-5t . \end{equation*}
  1. How tall is the tower?
  2. When does Jane reach the bottom?
  3. What is her velocity at time \(t\text{?}\)
  4. What is her speed at time \(t\text{?}\)
  5. What is her acceleration at time \(t\text{?}\)
  6. How far does she travel walking down the stairs?
