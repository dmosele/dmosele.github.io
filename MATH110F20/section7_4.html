Section 11.2 Graphs of Sine and Cosine

Worksheet 11.2.1 Worksheet

1.

Determine the midline and amplitude of the graph of \(y=3\sin(x)+1\) below.

Solution
The midline is \(y=1\) and the amplitude is 3.
2.

Determine the midline and amplitude of the graph of \(y=-2\cos(x)+4\) below.

Solution
The midline is \(y=4\) and the amplitude is 2. (Note that it isn't -2, because distances cannot be negative).
