Item 6.1.1.1.a.

To get the number of people in Forks in 1981 and 1982 we increase by 3.4% once and twice respectively.

\begin{equation*} \text{Pop in 1981} = 3060\cdot 1.034 \approx 3164 \text{ people} \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} \text{Pop in 1982} = 3060\cdot 1.034^2 \approx 3272 \text{ people} \end{equation*}
