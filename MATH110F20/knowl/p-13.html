\begin{equation*} \begin{array}{c|c|c|c|c|c|c} \text{Year} & 2010 & 2011 & 2012 & 2013 & 2014 & 2015 \\ \hline \text{Subscribers (in millions)} & 20.01 & 23.53 & 33.27 & 44.35 & 57.39 & 74.76 \\ \end{array} \end{equation*}
  1. Determine the rate of change of the number of Netflix subscribers between 2010 and 2013.
  2. Determine the rate of change of the number of Netflix subscribers between 2013 and 2015, and compare to you answer from part (a). What do you think this says about Netflix's growth?
